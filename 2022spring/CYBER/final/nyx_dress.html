2WB

nyx_dress

$40.00 USD


2WB is a digital fashion brand based in Ukraine, Kiev. 2WB is passionate about 3D fashion and wants to make a strong contribution into it along with other artists and designers developing the industry.

The brand’s first collection for DressX was inspired by the story of a future utopia in which humans have created an ‘Eden on Earth’  The ‘future-wear’ collection pays tribute to the elements of nature - soil, water, fire, air – through the garments, colors, shapes, and prints. The formation of the color palette is based on the main inspiration that was nature itself, so the colors are very diverse and reflect what we see around us.

dressx vision

We strongly believe that the amount of clothing produced today is way greater than humanity needs. We share the beauty and excitement that physical fashion creates, but we believe that there are ways to produce less, to produce more sustainably, and not to produce at all. At a current stage of DRESSX development, we aim to show that some clothes can exist only in their digital versions. Don't shop less, shop digital fashion.
